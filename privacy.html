Privacy Policy

Updated: 06/11/2025 – Privacy policy for TDStudio.

Summary

We only store account credentials (username + hashed password) and gameplay data (progress, items, achievements). We do not collect email or phone numbers, and we do not integrate any advertising SDKs.

Scope

This policy applies to all iOS releases of the TDStudio Game published by Thanh Di’s Gaming Studio.

Data We Process

  • User ID: username plus password stored as a secure hash (never in plain text).
  • Gameplay: progress, characters, and items stored on our servers for synchronization.
  • Diagnostics (optional): crash logs and performance metrics used solely to fix bugs and improve stability.

Your Rights & Choices

You can request account and data deletion at any time. Local data is removed when you uninstall the app; server-side data is deleted upon a verified request.

Contact

Email: contact@thanhdi.top · Form: thanhdi.top/contact.html

Policy Changes

If future versions require additional data or SDKs, we will update this page prior to release. Material changes will be communicated in-app before they take effect.
